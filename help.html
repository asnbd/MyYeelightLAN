Enable LAN Control

Do the following Steps to Enable LAN Control in Your Yeelight App

  1. Open Yeelight App.
  2. Choose your Device from the list.
  3. Press Up Arrow button located at the bottom-right of the screen.
  4. Tap on LAN Control from the popup menu.
  5. Enable LAN Control by pressing the switch.
That's all! Now open MyYeelightLAN App and Control Your Device more faster & without internet connectivity!
